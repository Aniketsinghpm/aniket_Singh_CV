FINTECH · AUTOMATION

Business Rules Engine (BRE) Integration in Loan Origination System

Enterslice Pvt. Ltd.  ·  2023–2024

🔴 Problem

The existing Loan Origination System (LOS) relied heavily on manual credit decisioning. Loan officers had to review each application individually, causing high turnaround time (TAT), inconsistent decisions, human errors, and compliance risks. With 70K+ monthly applications, this was not scalable.

🟡 Approach

  • Mapped the end-to-end LOS workflow and identified 12 manual decision points
  • Conducted stakeholder interviews with credit team, tech leads, and compliance officers
  • Benchmarked competitor fintech platforms using BRE (Drools, IBM ODM)
  • Prioritized rules using RICE framework — credit score, income ratio, bureau checks
  • Created HLD/LLD documentation and collaborated with tech team on API integration

🔵 Solution

  • Integrated a configurable Business Rules Engine into the LOS pipeline
  • Automated credit decisioning for 80% of standard loan applications
  • Built a rule management dashboard for the credit team to update rules without dev effort
  • Implemented real-time bureau API calls (CIBIL/Experian) within the decision flow
  • Ran parallel testing (UAT) with 500+ loan applications before go-live

🟢 Impact & Results

  • Reduced Turnaround Time (TAT) by 20%
  • Reduced manual review effort by 60%
  • Cut product defects by 30% post UAT
  • Enabled scaling to 70K+ monthly applications
  • Achieved ₹50Cr+ in loan disbursals
TAT ↓ 20% Manual Work ↓ 60% Defects ↓ 30% ₹50Cr+ Disbursals 70K+ Monthly Apps

E-COMMERCE · AI · SUBSCRIPTION

AI-Powered Dairy Subscription E-commerce Platform

Enterslice Pvt. Ltd.  ·  2023

🔴 Problem

The dairy business faced high delivery inefficiencies, frequent subscription cancellations due to missed deliveries, and no reliable way to forecast demand. Manual subscription renewals were causing revenue leakage and poor customer retention.

🟡 Approach

  • Conducted user research — interviewed 40+ subscribers to understand pain points
  • Analysed delivery data to identify route inefficiencies and demand patterns
  • Mapped the subscription lifecycle to find drop-off points
  • Evaluated AI/ML demand forecasting models and route optimisation algorithms
  • Built product roadmap with phased delivery: Forecasting → Route Opt → Auto Renewals

🔵 Solution

  • Built AI-driven demand forecasting engine to predict daily delivery volumes
  • Integrated route optimisation algorithm to cluster deliveries geographically
  • Automated subscription renewal system with smart reminders and retry logic
  • Developed a customer-facing app with real-time delivery tracking
  • Created an operations dashboard for fleet managers to monitor all routes

🟢 Impact & Results

  • Delivery efficiency improved by 25%
  • Repeat purchases increased by 30%
  • Operational costs reduced by 18%
  • Subscription churn reduced by 22%
Delivery Efficiency ↑ 25% Repeat Purchases ↑ 30% Ops Cost ↓ 18% Churn ↓ 22%

GROWTH · RETENTION · DATA

User Growth & Retention Program — Referral & Rewards

Enterslice Pvt. Ltd.  ·  2023–2024

🔴 Problem

User acquisition costs were high and organic growth was stagnant. Retention rates were declining month-over-month with no structured program to incentivise repeat usage or referrals. The product lacked a loyalty loop.

🟡 Approach

  • Analysed user funnel data — identified top drop-off stages using SQL & Tableau
  • Segmented users by behaviour: new, active, dormant, churned
  • Researched referral mechanics from fintech leaders (Groww, Zerodha, Fi Money)
  • Defined success metrics: CAC, LTV, referral conversion rate, retention D30/D60
  • Ran A/B tests on reward structures — cashback vs. credit vouchers vs. fee waivers

🔵 Solution

  • Designed a tiered referral program — higher rewards for quality referrals
  • Launched loyalty reward system tied to product usage milestones
  • Built personalised re-engagement campaigns for dormant users
  • Created real-time analytics dashboard to track program performance
  • Integrated reward wallet feature into the core product UI

🟢 Impact & Results

  • User acquisition grew by 15%
  • Retention improved by 12%
  • Market penetration increased by 10%
  • Referral conversion rate reached 18%
Acquisition ↑ 15% Retention ↑ 12% Market Penetration ↑ 10% Referral CVR 18%
